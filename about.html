Sebastian Gutierrez - Wikipedia

Sebastian Gutierrez (Born March 14, 2001), is a QuestBridge Scholar at the University of Notre Dame studying Computer Science, an aspiring Software Engineer + Startup team member, and a lover of tech and learning who hopes to make a difference in the world.

Years Active: 2001-Present Based: Orange County, California

Sebastian's Upbringing
Sebastian is a first-generation/low-income college student and a first-generation American, born to a single mother who immigrated from Mexico at the age of 18.

His awareness of the difference in his life that having resources while growing up made has influenced him to want to do meaningful work that creates opportunity, and he hopes to use CS in his future to do good.

Sebastian's Aspirations for the Future

As someone who is value-driven and strives to be a force for good, as well as someone who loves growing, learning, feeling challenged, and being passionate about what he does, Sebastian's dream is to work for a startup company. He hopes that through dedicated and personal investemnt in a place with an honorable vision, he can get a step closer to the positive impact he hopes to have.
